FAQ

What if the music cue does not play?

Check your internet connection, confirm that audio is allowed on your device, and try again. Music cue playback depends on Apple Music catalog availability and network conditions, and some tracks may not offer a cue depending on country or catalog availability.

How do I restore the Remove Ads purchase?

Open the app's purchase or settings screen and tap the restore option if available. If the purchase still does not appear, contact support with your Apple receipt details.

How is song data updated?

Song content and quiz data can be refreshed remotely, so the app may receive updated catalogs without requiring a full release from the App Store.

How does Apple Music preview work?

The app uses short music cues at runtime for quiz gameplay only. For full listening, the app may guide you to Apple Music links.

Is CUE officially affiliated with Apple Music?

No. CUE is not officially affiliated with Apple Music or Apple Inc. The app uses Apple Music catalog previews only for quiz gameplay.

Does the app work offline?

Some locally cached screens may open, but gameplay and preview playback generally require network access because the app relies on remote content. Some tracks may not provide a preview depending on country or catalog availability.
