CUE: KPOP Memory Challenge is a quiz app that loads preview URLs from the Apple Music catalog at runtime and plays them for quiz purposes. We keep the policy simple and transparent.

What CUE does

CUE is a KPOP memory quiz app. It uses short Apple Music preview clips during gameplay so players can identify songs and artists. The app does not store or redistribute full music files.

  • CUE uses Apple Music preview only for gameplay based on song identification, not as background music or for independent listening.
  • Full-track listening continues in the Apple Music service.
  • Music metadata, previews, artwork, and trademarks belong to their respective rights holders.

Music data

Preview clips are used only while the app is running. CUE does not save, resell, or share full music files.

Advertising

CUE may use Google AdMob to provide advertising. Ad delivery may involve device and usage information handled by Google according to its policies. CUE does not require a direct user account sign-in.

Remove Ads

CUE may offer an in-app purchase called Remove Ads. Purchase state may be stored on your device and associated with your app account or store receipt when applicable.

Local storage

App settings, purchase state, and cached data may be stored on the device so the app can work smoothly and remember your preferences.

Ad settings

Device-level ad settings can be managed through Apple or Google platform settings.
