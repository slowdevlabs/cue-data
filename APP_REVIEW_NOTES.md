# CUE App Review Notes

- CUE uses Apple Music preview clips strictly for quiz identification gameplay.
- The app does not cache, redistribute, or provide full music streaming.
- Users can continue listening through official Apple Music links.
- Music previews are loaded dynamically at runtime from Apple Music catalog data.
- The app is not affiliated with Apple Music or Apple Inc.
- Music metadata, previews, artwork, and trademarks remain with their respective rights holders.
